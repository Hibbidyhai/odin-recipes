Air Fryer Brussel Sprouts

Home

Description

Follow this quick and easy recipe to create crispy and flavorful brussels sprouts using an air fryer.

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook time: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds Brussels sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
    • <1i>1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Preheat air fryer to 390 degrees fahrenheit (200 degrees celsius) for 15 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, trim Brussels sprouts. Add olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper; toss well.
  5. Spread sprouts evenly in the air fryer basket.
  6. Cook in the preheated air fryer for 15 minutes, checking and shaking the basket halfway through the cycle.
  7. Serve and enjoy!s
