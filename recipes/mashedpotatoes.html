Slow Cooker Mashed Potatoes

Home

Description

Follow this quick and easy recipe to create delicious and creamy mashed potatoes using a slow cooker and just a handful of ingredients.

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook time: 2 hrs 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds of red potatoes
  • 3 cubes of chicken bouillon
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 8 ounces of sour cream
    • <1i>1/2 cup of butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients
  2. Place potatoes, bouillon, and garlic in a large pot and cover with water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
  4. Drain in a colander over a large bowl, reserving cooking water.
  5. Mash potatoes with sour cream and cream cheese until smooth and creamy, adding a little reserved water if need to reach desired consistency.
  6. Transfer mashed potato mixture to a slow cooker, cover, and cook on Low for 2 to 3 hours.
  7. Just before serving, stir in butter and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!s
